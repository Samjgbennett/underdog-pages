This Privacy Policy explains how UnderDog ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the UnderDog mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

  • Account information: the email address you provide when you create an account. Passwords are handled securely by our authentication provider (Google Firebase) and are never stored by us in readable form.
  • App activity: the dogs you save to your matches, so we can show them to you across sessions.
  • Content you submit: if you are an approved organisation, the listing details and images you upload.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App (authentication, saving matches, showing listings).
  • To respond to support requests and moderate reported content.
  • To keep the App safe and enforce our Terms of Use.

How We Store and Share Data

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage). We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties except as required to operate the App or comply with the law.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App: open the Account menu (person icon) and choose Delete Account. You may also email us to request deletion. Once deleted, your account information and saved matches are permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
