UnderDog Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

UnderDog ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dog adoption browsing app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is stored.

What we collect

  • Account information: When you sign in, we store your email address and a unique user ID via Firebase Authentication.
  • Liked dogs: Dogs you swipe right on are saved to your account in Firebase Firestore so your Matches list persists across devices.
  • Submitted photos: If you submit a dog for adoption, any photo you choose from your library is uploaded to Firebase Storage.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, location, payment details, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-party services

UnderDog uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and file storage

Data retention

Your account data (liked dogs) is retained until you delete your account or request deletion. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the address below.

Children

UnderDog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.
