Frequently Asked Questions

What is UnderDog?
UnderDog is a swipe-to-discover app that helps you find rescue and shelter dogs available for adoption near you. Swipe right to save a dog to your matches, then tap "Fetch" to visit the rescue's page and start the adoption process.

How do I report an inappropriate listing?
Open any dog and tap the flag / report icon, or use the block option, to flag content you believe is objectionable. You can also email us directly. We review every report within 24 hours.

How do I delete my account?
In the app, open the account menu (person icon, top right) and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. You can also email us to request deletion.

How is a dog added to the app?
Rescue organisations and charities can register through our onboarding form to list dogs in their care. All listings are subject to our content policy.
